What is the process of renting a car?

Choose a car, provide necessary documents (license & ID), select rental duration, make a payment, and pick up the vehicle.

can i rent a car at 18 years old?

Yes, you can rent a car at 18 in some locations, but there are restrictions. Most rental companies require renters to be at least 21 years old, but some allow 18-year-olds, especially in certain states or for specific vehicle categories.
