Overview of Hector-dual-tone

The MG Hector Dual-Tone is a stylish and feature-rich mid-size SUV that stands out with its bold design, premium features, and connected technology. As a variant of the popular MG Hector, the dual-tone edition enhances the SUV’s appeal with a contrasting roof color, giving it a sportier and more dynamic look. The exterior design includes a chrome-studded front grille, LED DRLs, LED headlamps, and muscular body lines. The dual-tone roof, usually available in black or white, adds a distinctive touch to the SUV. Inside, the cabin is spacious and loaded with modern technology, featuring a 14-inch touchscreen infotainment system, wireless Android Auto & Apple CarPlay, a fully digital instrument cluster, ventilated seats, and a panoramic sunroof. Under the hood, the MG Hector Dual-Tone is powered by a 1.5L turbo-petrol engine (with mild-hybrid technology) and a 2.0L diesel engine. Transmission options include a 6-speed manual, CVT, and DCT automatic..
