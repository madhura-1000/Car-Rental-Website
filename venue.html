Overview of Hyndai-Venue

The Hyundai Venue is a compact SUV known for its bold design, advanced technology, and efficient performance. Launched in 2019, it quickly gained popularity in markets like India, competing with models such as the Tata Nexon, Maruti Brezza, and Kia Sonet. The Venue is positioned as a stylish and urban-friendly SUV, ideal for city driving as well as highway commutes. The Venue features a striking front grille, LED projector headlamps, and a sporty stance. The cabin is modern and packed with technology, including an 8-inch touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, a digital driver’s display, automatic climate control, a wireless charger, and a sunroof in higher variants. Under the hood, the Venue offers multiple engine options, including a 1.2L naturally aspirated petrol, a 1.0L turbo-petrol, and a 1.5L diesel. Transmission choices include a 5-speed manual, 6-speed manual, iMT (clutchless manual), and 7-speed DCT (automatic), catering to different driving preferences. For safety, the Venue comes with six airbags, ABS with EBD, electronic stability control, and advanced driver assistance features (ADAS) in top variants. With Hyundai’s strong after-sales support and feature-loaded variants, the Venue is a well-rounded option in the compact SUV segment.
