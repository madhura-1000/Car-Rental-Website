Overview of Maruti-alto

The Maruti Suzuki Alto is one of India’s most popular and affordable hatchbacks, known for its compact size, fuel efficiency, and budget-friendly pricing. Since its launch in 2000, the Alto has been a best-seller in the entry-level car segment, making it a favorite among first-time car buyers. It competes with models like the Renault Kwid and Hyundai Santro. The latest-generation Alto features a compact yet modern design, with a bold front grille, swept-back headlights, and a refreshed bumper. The cabin is simple yet functional, offering features like a 7-inch SmartPlay infotainment system with Android Auto and Apple CarPlay, power windows, keyless entry, and ample legroom for a small hatchback. Under the hood, the Alto is powered by a 796cc petrol engine or a 1.0L K-Series engine (in the Alto K10 variant), offering excellent fuel efficiency. It comes with a 5-speed manual transmission and an AMT (Automatic Gear Shift) option for added convenience. A CNG variant is also available for eco-conscious buyers.
