Overview of Maruti-Baleno

The Maruti Suzuki Baleno is a premium hatchback known for its stylish design, spacious cabin, fuel efficiency, and advanced features. First introduced in 2015, the Baleno quickly became one of India’s best-selling hatchbacks, competing with models like the Hyundai i20, Tata Altroz, and Toyota Glanza. The latest-generation Baleno features a sleek front grille, LED projector headlamps, and a sculpted body that gives it a premium look. The cabin is modern and spacious, offering a 9-inch SmartPlay Pro+ infotainment system, wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, a head-up display (HUD), cruise control, and a 360-degree camera in higher variants. Under the hood, the Baleno is powered by a 1.2L K-Series Dual Jet petrol engine with mild-hybrid technology, delivering a balance of power and fuel efficiency. It is available with a 5-speed manual transmission and an AGS (AMT) automatic for convenience. The car is well-known for its high mileage, making it a favorite among budget-conscious buyers. Safety has been improved with six airbags, ABS with EBD, electronic stability control (ESC), and ISOFIX child seat anchors. With Maruti’s strong after-sales service and a feature-loaded package, the Baleno remains one of the most preferred premium hatchbacks in India.
